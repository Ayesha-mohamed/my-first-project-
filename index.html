story of the perfume

Bleu de Chanel was created by Jacques Polge, Chanel's master perfumer,
as a bold expression of a man who refuses to be bound by convention.
Inspired by independence and inner confidence, it blends fresh citrus,
rich woods, and aromatic notes. Its deep blue bottle symbolizes mystery,
depth, and masculinity. The scent gained massive popularity for
its versatility fresh enough for daytime, yet deep and sensual for the night.
It became an icon of timeless sophistication, and later evolved into Eau de Parfum
(2014) and Parfum (2018) versions. It’s not just a perfume — it's a statement of power and refinement.
